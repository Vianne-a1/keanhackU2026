Registration Flow

Choose whether you are creating an organization or joining one.

Organization accounts create a workspace and approval authority. User accounts search for a company and wait for that organization to verify the request.

Create an Organization

Use this path if you are setting up a new company workspace.

  • Become the first admin for the organization.
  • Upload policy documents and manage access.
  • Approve future user requests for your workspace.

Join an Organization

Use this path if your company already exists and you need access.

  • Search or type your organization name.
  • Submit your work email and full name.
  • Wait for org approval before login is enabled.
