Red Flag Sample Audit Result
  • Automatic 5-year renewal without opt-out clause.
  • New payment destination differs from previous invoices.
  • Additional service fees not present in original quote.

Risk Categories Checked

  • Ghost services or undefined deliverables.
  • Unexpected payment term modifications.
  • Bank account changes from historical records.
